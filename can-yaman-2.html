Wiki/Biography

Can Yaman was born on Wednesday, 8 November 1989 (age 32 years; as of 2021) in Suadiye, Istanbul in Turkey. His zodiac sign is Scorpio.

A childhood picture of Can Yaman with his mother

He did his schooling at Bilfen Schools, İstanbul, Turkey and continued his schooling at Liceo Italiano IMI, Istanbul, Turkey. From 2003 to 2008, he attended Özel İtalyan Lisesi, a school in Istanbul, Turkey. He did his graduation in law from Yeditepe University, Istanbul, Turkey, from 2008 to 2012 on scholarship. In the student exchange program, he studied in America during his graduation. [1] [2]

A picture of Can Yaman from his graduation days

He moved from Turkey to Italy in January 2021.

Family

He was born to an Albanian family. [3] In an interview, while talking about his descent. He said,

On the part of my father I am of Albanian descent from Prishtina. My grandfather grew up in Pristina, my grandmother in Skopje. In 1959 we came to Istanbul. Father’s relatives speak Albanian. Distant cousins. Because I did not meet them I do not know them. Relatives like the father of my father, grandmother’s brothers. They speak Albanian. My grandfather and my grandmother also speak Albanian. Even, there are cases that speak Albanian. But I grew older by the mother. And there we did not speak Albanian, we speak only Turkish. That’s why I do not know Albanian. Neither does my dad know, my uncle knows. He was born in Pristina. There are 5 brothers, after him others were born in Turkey. I went to Albania, Tirana and stayed almost a month there. In Albania everyone knew Italian and I spoke Italian.”

Career

Law

In July 2011, he started working as an intern (Stajyer in Turkish) with the Turkish lawyer Somay Hukuk Burosu in Istanbul, Turkey. In February 2014, he joined the Turkish attorney PricewaterhouseCoopers, Turkey. He then worked as a lawyer at Istanbul Bar Association, Turkey. He has also worked in mergers and acquisitions firm and wrote articles for the tax section of the newspaper ‘Dunya.’ After working in the corporate law sector for a few years, he realised that he was not meant to work in the corporate sector. In an interview with a magazine, while talking about the same. He said,

I felt like having an open view but sitting in an office and wearing a suit bothered me a lot. When they gave me an annual leave, I left without coming back. It was on TV that my colleagues saw me next.”

He has a law office in partnership along with his two friends in Kartal, Turkey.

Acting

While he was on a summer trip, he met the Turkish acting managers Cuneyt Sayil and Ilker Bilgi. They offered Can a role in a Turkish series, and in 2014, Can debuted as a supporting actor in the Turkish TV series ‘Gönül İşleri’ in which he played the role of Bedir.

A still from the TV series ‘Gönül İşleri’

He then played the lead role of Yalın Aras in the Turkish TV series ‘İnadına Aşk’ (2015).

İnadına Aşk poster

He was then roped in the Turkish TV series ‘Hangimiz Sevmedik’ (2016) and ‘Dolunay’ (2017).

‘Dolunay’ (2017)

He gained immense popularity with his role as Can Divit in the Turkish TV series ‘Erkenci Kuş’ in 2018. Later, the series was dubbed in various languages like Hindi, English, and Tamil. It is considered one of the best Turkish TV series to be dubbed in different languages.

Other Work

Apart from working as a lawyer and actor, he has also appeared in a few modelling projects. He is a philanthropist too and has been associated with a charity campaign ‘Can Yaman For Children.’ In an interview, while talking about an NGO for children, he said,

Then there is the non-profit association that I created to help less fortunate children. I care about children, I want to help the little ones who are in poor conditions. Childhood, adolescence was fundamental periods for my growth.”

Controversies

Insulting the Co-Star

In 2016, while he was shooting for the Turkish TV series ‘Hangimiz Sevmedik’ he threw a teacup at his co-actor Selen Soyder and insulted her. A case was filed against both the actors, and after hearing the witnesses and checking the camera footage, Can was found to be guilty. He was then fined a judicial fine of 7 thousand 830 Turkish lira by the local police. [4]

Controversial Remark on Demet Özdemir

In 2020, he made a controversial remark for his co-star Demet Özdemir. [5] In an interview, he said,

I have sex a lot, so I am not pent to talk about it constantly. I cannot hide my feelings towards Özdemir, I am obsessed with the idea of making love to her and is not ashamed of talking about it. Other fellow actors are upset because I am telling the truth. If I had told what was happening behind the scenes of their series, I would have exposed them, but I do not interfere in the affairs of others.”

Can continued,

I did not insult Demet. I contacted her and explained my intention and she understood. The intimate desire does not mean sex itself, but rather a series of beautiful feelings in which any one is attracted to the other, something less than love and greater than friendship.”
